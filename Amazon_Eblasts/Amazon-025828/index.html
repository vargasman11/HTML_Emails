What is Amazon’s Post A Truck?

  • Your company can post available empty capacity by specifying details such as when and where trucks are available, one-way/round-trip, and a minimum payout.
  • The Post A Truck page has new filtering capabilities that allows your company to set the maximum number of stops, exclude cities from your Post A Truck availability, and for one-ways you can select multiple destination cities or regions (i.e., Northeast).
  • If a Trip on Relay Load Board matches the availability your company posted and has a payout equal to or higher than the minimum payout that was entered, Amazon can automatically book the freight for your company.
